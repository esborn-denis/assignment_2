HISTORY

Nakuru is a city in the Rift Valley region of Kenya. It is the capital of Nakuru County, and is the third largest urban area in Kenya. As of 2019, Nakuru has an urban population of 570,674,[2] making it the largest urban centre in the Rift Valley, succeeding Eldoret, Uasin Gishu County

The city lies along the Nairobi–Nakuru Highway, 160 kilometres (99 mi) from Nairobi.
